LaTeX Examples with MathJax

Inline Math

This is an inline LaTeX equation: \( E = mc^2 \). It shows how energy is related to mass and the speed of light.

Block Math

The quadratic formula:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

Fractions and Square Roots

Here is an example of a fraction and a square root:

$$\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}$$

Summations and Integrals

A summation:

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}$$

An integral:

$$\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} \, dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}$$

Matrix Example

Here's an example of a 2x2 matrix:

$$\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}$$

More Complex Equation

Here's a more complex example that combines several LaTeX elements:

$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi} $$
